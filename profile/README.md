# PivotMarkets AI

**AI workflow automation, grant intelligence, workforce transformation, and enterprise AI implementation.**

Pivot Markets International, LLC is a US-based company building AI-powered tools for American businesses, workforce agencies, and municipalities. We provide training, consultation, and workflow implementation for organizations adopting AI across multiple industries.



## About

We started with a simple problem: general contractors in the US are losing money because their financial data lives in disconnected systems. That problem led us to build CashFlowSmart — a financial operating system that connects estimating, invoicing, bookkeeping, and cash flow forecasting into one platform.

From there, we expanded into a broader mission. Small businesses, workforce agencies, libraries, and local governments across the US are all struggling with the same challenge: they know AI matters, but they have no idea how to implement it without burning money on tools nobody knows how to use.

We solve that.



## What We Build

**CashFlowSmart.io** — Financial operating system for US contractors. Connects with Contractor Plus for estimating, HardLedger for bookkeeping, and Plaid for bank feeds. AI-powered cash flow forecasting, job costing, and margin analysis. Currently in development with an active pilot partner.

**HardLedger** — Bookkeeping platform built for contractors. Live and in production. Balance sheets, income statements, cash flow reports, receipt matching, and CSV import. Deployed at msc-accounting.com.

**Sovereign Workflow Lab** — AI training and workflow platform designed for public libraries, workforce agencies, and chambers of commerce. Regional hub model with grant compliance built in. Backend complete with lead capture, grant mapping logic, and regional configuration for Indiana and Ohio.

**Logic Architect Framework** — Our methodology for workforce AI transformation. Includes aptitude assessment, sovereign workflow training, employer-validated certification, and job placement. Designed to work within existing state and federal grant programs.


## Current Operations

We are actively working across multiple US states on AI workforce initiatives:

- **Indiana** — Regional economic hubs under READI 2.0. Partnership with Nappanee Public Library. Focus on manufacturing workforce upskilling.
- **North Carolina** — DLI workforce grant application targeting rural recovery in Robeson and Edgecombe counties.
- **Pennsylvania** — CODE PA and InnovateUS state staff upskilling programs.
- **Kentucky** — Career centers and re-entry programs for unemployed workers.
- **Michigan** — Manufacturing workforce reskilling through Michigan Works and MEDC.
- **Ohio** — Regional workforce development through Columbus hub.

Our institutional approach covers four channels: chambers of commerce for small business readiness audits, public libraries for community AI literacy, workforce and unemployment offices for re-entry training, and high schools and junior colleges for career track development.
